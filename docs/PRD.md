# Product Requirements Document: Mail Tuner

## 1. Problem Statement
Managing email configurations across multiple accounts, services, and applications is a complex and error-prone process. Users often struggle with:
- Inconsistent settings across different email clients and services
- Difficulty backing up and restoring email configurations
- Time spent manually recreating email settings when switching devices or reinstalling software
- Lack of centralized management for both personal and professional email accounts
